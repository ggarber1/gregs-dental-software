# Carestream Dental / Sensei (Carestream Health / Envista Holdings)

## Overview

**Company:** Carestream Dental (standalone since 2017; recapitalized Sept 2024 via General Atlantic Credit — $525M)
**Products:** SoftDent, PracticeWorks (legacy server); WinOMS (oral surgery); OrthoTrac (orthodontics); Sensei Cloud (current generation)
**Note:** Carestream's intraoral scanner business (CS 3600/3700/3800) was sold to Envista Holdings/DEXIS for $600M in April 2022
**Market position:** Top-5 global dental PMS player; particularly strong in imaging-integrated workflows and specialty practices
**Capterra rating (SoftDent/legacy):** ~2.9/5 (76 reviews) — one of the lowest in the category
**Capterra rating (Sensei Cloud):** Mixed early feedback

## Pricing

Carestream Dental does **not publicly publish pricing** for any practice management product. Entirely quote-driven.

### Third-Party Estimates
| Practice Size | Est. Monthly (per user) |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Small (1–5 users) | ~$400/user/month |
| Medium (~10 users) | ~$350/user/month |
| Large (~100 users) | $250–$300/user/month |

### CS Imaging 8 (the only publicly purchasable item)
- **$49/month** on a 12-month contract; auto-renews month-to-month
- Includes: phone support, updates

### Sensei Cloud
- Not publicly disclosed
- **Critical complaint:** Users report pricing nearly **doubling within the first month** of onboarding due to undisclosed add-ons not included in quoted price

### Training
- Live remote training available at hourly rates or in three-hour bundles
- Specific rates: contact SalesCoordinators.US@csdental.com
- Free quarterly webinars for practices with valid support agreements

### Implementation
- Not publicly disclosed; varies by product and practice size

### Contract Terms
- **CS Imaging 8:** 12-month initial contract; auto-renews month-to-month
- Other products: individual master service agreements (not public)
- Carestream can raise subscription prices with **30 days' notice** before customer's non-renewal deadline — only remedy is cancellation
- Certain subscription fees are **non-cancelable and non-refundable**
- Cancellation: must send Non-Renewal Notice to CustomerServiceOperations@csdental.com before deadline

## Products & Features

### CS SoftDent (Legacy — General Practice, Server-Based)
- Scheduling, patient records, clinical charting
- Treatment planning
- Billing, claims management, insurance processing
- Document management
- Add-on modules: patient engagement, imaging integration
- Patient Manager add-on (patient engagement)
- Digital intake forms

### CS PracticeWorks (Legacy — Small/Medium General Practice, Server-Based)
- Core scheduling, billing, patient communication
- Claims processing
- Treatment planning and charting
- Add-on modules available

### CS OrthoTrac (Orthodontic Specialty — Server-Based)
- Orthodontic-specific treatment card with aligner compliance tracking
- Treatment charting and progress tracking
- Drag-and-drop scheduling across multiple chairs and locations
- Automated billing and patient communication
- Patient recall and automated reminders
- Multi-location scheduling

### CS WinOMS (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery — Server-Based)
- **Medical and dental insurance billing with automatic cross-coding** — a unique differentiator
- Referral management and scheduling
- **Anesthesia records** (pre/post eval, vital signs, drug tracking)
- Document management (consent forms, medical history, surgical history)
- OMS-specific clinical workflows

### Sensei Cloud (Current Generation — Cloud)
**Scheduling**
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Multi-provider scheduling
- Reminders and task assignment system (Sept 2024)

**Clinical**
- Charting, treatment planning, clinical notes
- Specialty expansion: oral surgery anesthesia records, ortho workflows added (2024)
- Pharmacy preference management (Oct 2024)
- Vitals Monitor Integration for anesthesia/oral surgery (Aug 2024)

**Billing**
- Claims management
- Patient payment processing
- **Limitation:** Billing module described as needing "complete overhaul" by users

**Imaging**
- 3Shape Unite intraoral scanner integration (Sept 2024)
- iTero intraoral scanner integration (Feb 2024)
- BeamReaders radiology consultation via Click & Share (2024)
- Pearl Second Opinion AI integration (Jan 2025) — real-time pathology detection

**Patient Engagement**
- Automated reminders
- Digital forms

**Reporting**
- Acknowledged as incomplete; users report as a current weakness

## Deployment

### Legacy Products (SoftDent, PracticeWorks, WinOMS, OrthoTrac)
- Server-based, on-premise (Windows)
- "SoftDent Hosted" option: Carestream-hosted server; practice avoids local server maintenance
- **Performance issue:** Works best on older Windows configurations; modern Windows causes severe degradation

### Sensei Cloud
- Fully cloud-based; no local server required
- Any internet-connected device; automatic updates and backups
- **Sensei Cloud Mobile app** exists (distinct EULA)
- Connected hardware: imaging systems, scanners

### CS Imaging Platform
- Primarily local/network-based installation; hybrid cloud options

| Aspect | Legacy | Sensei Cloud |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Server required | Yes | No |
| Cloud | No (hosted option) | Yes |
| Mobile | No | Yes (Sensei Cloud Mobile) |
| Offline | Partial (local server) | No |

## User Reviews

### SoftDent / PracticeWorks (Legacy) — Capterra ~2.9/5
**Praise:**
- Familiarity after long-term use
- Adequate for simple workflows when stable

**Complaints:**
- **Crashes frequently; images take up to 2 minutes to load**
- **UI looks like Windows XP** — older hardware actually runs it FASTER than newer hardware
- Bugs in every update
- "Recommended to avoid" by multiple longtime users

### Sensei Cloud — Mixed Early Feedback
**Positive:**
- Cloud access appreciated
- Cleaner interface than legacy products

**Negative:**
- **Pricing nearly doubled** from quoted price due to undisclosed add-ons
- Schedule sync issues between machines requiring manual refresh
- **Data save failures** leading to duplicate records
- Entire modules that don't function
- **Billing described as needing a "complete overhaul"**
- Imaging module lacks basic functionality
- **"Staff morale destroyed"** by the switch — one practice owner's words
- Reporting described as incomplete and confusing

## Customer Support — Systemic Failure

This is Carestream Dental's most consistently cited weakness across all products:

- **Phone hold times:** 30 minutes to over 1 hour to reach Level 1; most issues escalate to Level 2 (additional wait)
- **Callback delays:** 2-week wait for callbacks regardless of urgency
- **No after-hours emergency support**
- **Written contact:** Complaints filed with no response for months
- **Training quality:** All online; no in-person option; users unable to operate hardware after training because it was entirely verbal with no visual component
- **Positive outliers:** A minority praise tech support agents as knowledgeable and patient when reached, particularly for SoftDent
- **Pissed Consumer profile:** Consistently negative — customers unable to reach anyone; no follow-through on refunds

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Legacy products are technically obsolete** — SoftDent best on Windows XP architecture; modern Windows causes severe degradation
2. **Sensei Cloud is incomplete** — billing module inadequate; reporting confusing; modules that don't function; data save reliability issues
3. **Customer support is a systemic failure** — 1-hour hold times; 2-week callback delays; no emergency channel
4. **Pricing opacity with hidden costs** — Sensei Cloud pricing doubles due to undisclosed add-ons
5. **Fragmented product portfolio** — confusing which product to buy; legacy-to-cloud migration path unclear
6. **Training entirely online and inadequate** — no in-person option; users can't operate products after training
7. **Image load times on legacy** — up to 2 minutes per X-ray
8. **Updates break things** — new releases introduce new bugs regularly
9. **No meaningful financial management tools** — lacks integrated patient payment history depth
10. **Staff morale impact** — migration from familiar legacy products is disruptive without adequate support
11. **Vendor split risk** — hardware (DEXIS/Envista) and software (Carestream) are now separate companies
