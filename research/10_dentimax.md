# DentiMax (DentiMax, LLC)

## Overview

**Company:** DentiMax, LLC
**Market position:** Frequently listed in top 10 dental PMS by Capterra and Software Advice; known as the easiest-to-use option for small practices with proprietary imaging hardware
**Products:** DentiMax Legacy (server/on-premise) + DentiMax Flow (cloud, launched 2024)
**Capterra rating:** 4.5/5 (94 reviews)
**Key differentiator:** One of the few vendors with both PMS software AND proprietary dental X-ray sensors — tight hardware/software integration

---

## Pricing

DentiMax operates two distinct product lines with separate pricing structures.

### DentiMax Legacy (Server/On-Premise)
| Tier | Monthly Price | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| In-Office (base) | $169/month | Year 1; drops to ~$129/month in Year 2 |
| Core | $299/month | Full practice management |
| Executive | $549/month | Expanded features |
| Unlimited | $669/month | All features, unlimited users |
| Ultimate Bundle | $328–$358/month | Tech support + training included |

### DentiMax Flow (Cloud/Browser-Based — launched 2024)
| Tier | Monthly Price | Inclusions |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Flow Starter | ~$199/month | Entry-level; limited seats |
| Flow Core | $699/month | 5 users; Starter Cloud Imaging; 8 hrs training; eClaims bundle (eAttachments, Realtime Eligibility, Realtime Claim Status) |
| Flow Executive | $899/month | Unlimited Cloud Imaging; Unlimited eClaims bundle; 10 hrs training; Patient Education license; eRx Services |

### Add-On / Hardware Costs
- Pedo Sensor (digital X-ray): **$2,999**
- Adult Sensor: **$3,999**
- Market sensors typically range $3,000–$10,000 total
- Annual sensor support fees vary by provider (some as high as $1,000/sensor/year)
- Implementation (software): ~$500–$1,000 for small practices; up to $5,000 for larger

### What's Included Free
- Free installation
- Two remote imaging training sessions (1 hour each) at setup
- Follow-up training session after initial use period

### Contract Terms
- Monthly subscription (recurring payment due 1st of month)
- 10-day grace period before suspension for non-payment
- 60-day delinquency = permanent disconnection
- 14-day return window for hardware from installation; physical return required within 30 days of shipping label
- Month-to-month appears standard for software (no long-term lock-in disclosed)

## Features

### Scheduling
- Appointment book with custom scheduler views
- Revenue goal tracking per day/provider
- Appointment templating
- Online appointment booking (via add-on partners)
- Appointment reminders via SMS/text

### Clinical
- Customizable clinical charting
- Perio and PSR (Periodontal Screening and Recording) charting
- Perio Exam Screen with graphical comparisons over time
- Treatment planning module
- Templated clinical notes
- Patient education integration (Executive tier and above)
- eRx (electronic prescribing) — included in Flow Executive; add-on otherwise

### Billing & Financial
- Line-item accounting
- Electronic insurance claims (eClaims via DentalXChange)
- Electronic attachments for claims
- Realtime eligibility verification
- Realtime claim status tracking
- Insurance payment posting
- Patient statements
- Payment processing via Prahsys partnership
- Employee time tracking

### Imaging — Key Differentiator
- Proprietary DentiMax Imaging Software — **one of the few fully open imaging systems**
- Supports **25+ third-party sensor brands** (Schick, Dexis, Carestream, etc.) — uniquely hardware-agnostic
- X-rays integrated directly into charting screen
- Cloud imaging available (tiered: Starter vs. Unlimited in Flow)
- New 1.5 Open Sensor (April 2024): 11% smaller than predecessor; improved image quality
- CBCT (3D) scanners via Owandy partnership (2025): 2D, 3D, and 3D Ceph models

### Patient Engagement
- SMS appointment reminders
- Patient communication tools (via partner integrations)
- Electronic patient registration/intake forms
- Patient kiosk (reported as frequently non-functional)

### Reporting
- Practice analytics and reporting (standard)
- **Customization described as limited** by users
- Revenue tracking by provider/date range

### AI Features (DentiMax Flow 2.0, 2025)
- AI-assisted X-ray reading for early problem detection
- Voice command support for hands-free record updating

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Solo dentists, independent general practitioners, small-to-mid practices
- **Secondary:** DSOs and multi-location groups (Flow is positioned here)
- **Also serves:** Startups, mobile dentistry units, independent hygienists, dental billers, nonprofit dental organizations
- **Specialty:** Primarily general dentistry; no specialty-specific modules advertised
- **Not well-suited:** High-volume specialty practices needing specialty-specific charting

## Deployment

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Legacy (DentiMax) | Windows-based, installed on local server; practice owns hardware |
| Flow (DentiMax Flow) | Browser-based, true cloud-native; accessible from any device (launched 2024) |
| Mobile | No dedicated iOS/Android app; Flow's browser access provides mobile compatibility |
| OS | Windows primary for legacy; Flow is platform-agnostic via browser |
| IT requirement | Legacy requires server; Flow eliminates this |

## User Reviews

### Praise
- **Affordable pricing** compared to Dentrix/Eaglesoft
- **Tight integration between proprietary imaging hardware and PMS** — unique differentiator
- US-based support staff described as knowledgeable when reached
- Easy to learn for new staff
- **Transparent pricing model**
- eClaims integration (DentalXChange) praised as making insurance "a breeze"
- Long-term users (8+ years) report consistent reliability

### Complaints
- **Support phone system is a single queue** — users report being on hold then disconnected
- Must wait weeks to get non-critical issues resolved
- Software freezing — company often blames Windows updates
- **Kiosk feature frequently non-functional**
- **Rounding bug** — 1-cent balance discrepancy frustrates billing staff
- Treatment planning page uses outdated terminology
- **Claim resubmission requires starting from scratch** — cannot correct and resend
- Limited report customization
- Mobile functionality limited
- Transition/onboarding described as rough by some users
- Company changed security settings without user notification

## Customer Support

- **Channels:** Phone (single line), email (ticket system), chat, knowledge base
- **Callback feature:** Phone system offers a "hold your place in line" callback option
- **Email:** support@dentimax.com generates ticket with callback
- **Self-reported:** 9/10 customer support score
- **Reality from reviews:** Great staff when reached — the bottleneck is access. Single phone queue = long waits and disconnections. Non-critical issues can take weeks to schedule.
- No 24/7 support

## Integration Ecosystem

DentiMax explicitly aims to reduce third-party reliance by consolidating features natively.

| Category | Partners |
|----------|---------|
| eClaims / Eligibility | DentalXChange (eClaims, eAttachments, eligibility) |
| Payments | Prahsys |
| CBCT/3D imaging | Owandy (2025 partnership) |
| Patient communication | Unnamed partners |
| Patient education | Video partners |
| Imaging hardware | 25+ sensor brands (Schick, Dexis, Carestream, and others) |

**Notable gaps:**
- No native accounting software integration (QuickBooks, etc.)
- Limited open API ecosystem compared to larger competitors
- Fewer patient engagement/marketing platform integrations

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Support bottleneck** — single phone queue; long waits; disconnections
2. **Persistent bugs** — rounding errors, freezing, kiosk failures
3. **Limited report customization** — frustrating for data-driven practices
4. **Claim resubmission workflow** — complete restart required instead of correction
5. **Outdated treatment planning terminology**
6. **Mobile experience** — browser-based only; no native app
7. **Integration gaps** — limited accounting and patient engagement tool connections
8. **Transition/onboarding pain** — known rough spots when migrating from other software
9. **Flow is new and unproven** — limited production track record as of 2024 launch
10. **Security change without user notification** — verified complaint
