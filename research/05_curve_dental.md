# Curve Dental / Curve SuperHero (Curve Dental, Inc.)

## Overview

**Company:** Curve Dental, Inc. (backed by Battery Ventures, invested 2018)
**Market position:** ~0.63% market share by install count; ~$24.2M ARR; ~161 employees; one of the fastest-growing cloud-native platforms
**Product:** Curve SuperHero (all-in-one suite launched 2025)
**G2 rating:** 4.6/5 (273 reviews — largest review base among cloud dental platforms)
**Capterra rating:** 4.4/5
**Architecture:** 100% cloud-native (AWS)

## Pricing

Curve Dental advertises "transparent pricing" but **does not publish fixed rates** — all pricing is quote-based.

### What's Included (all-in-one model)
Base subscription reportedly includes: scheduling, charting, billing, imaging, patient engagement, reporting, and business analytics — reducing need for most add-ons.

### Notable Add-ons / New Paid Features (2025)
- **Curve Pay:** Integrated payment processing (launched Sept 2025); setup + terminal fees waived through 2025 for early adopters; per-transaction fees apply (rates not published)
- **Eligibility+:** AI insurance verification (launched Aug 2025); pricing not disclosed; reduces verification time by up to 95%
- **Curve FLO AI:** Suite of AI tools (Ask CurveAI, Call Summaries, Voice-to-Note, AI X-Ray Diagnostics); bundle/add-on pricing unclear

### Implementation
- Curve's team handles data conversion from existing systems
- Implementation 360: structured 5-step onboarding for DSOs and group practices
- Costs not publicly disclosed; typically bundled or included for larger accounts

## Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop scheduling with color coding and visual customization
- Online appointment booking for patients
- Automated appointment confirmations and reminders
- Multi-location scheduling with centralized view
- Curve Mobile: schedule management on iOS/Android

### Clinical
- Charting (tooth chart, treatment planning)
- Periodontal charting (noted as a weakness for complex perio workflows)
- **Voice-to-Note:** Bola AI integration for SOAP-formatted clinical notes via dictation
- **AI X-Ray Diagnostics:** Pearl AI integration
- Medical history and patient records
- Smart Forms: digital intake and consent forms completed by patients before appointment
- Electronic prescriptions

### Billing
- Electronic claims submission via DentalXChange and others
- Insurance eligibility verification (standard + Eligibility+ AI-powered)
- Treatment plan financial estimation
- Collections and A/R management
- **Centralized billing across all locations** (multi-location)
- Curve Pay: fully integrated payment processing (in-office, online, mobile); next-day payouts
- Buy Now Pay Later via Affirm integration
- Accepts HSA/FSA cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay

### Imaging
- Built-in cloud imaging: capture, store, view X-rays and intraoral images in the cloud
- Compatible with most digital X-ray sensors, intraoral cameras, and panoramic systems
- Pearl AI integration for AI-assisted radiographic diagnostics
- Images stored in same database as charts (unified record)
- **Known weakness:** Imaging regarded as less powerful than standalone tools like Dexis

### Patient Engagement
- Automated appointment confirmations, reminders, and recall messages
- Two-way patient texting (including image sharing) via Curve Mobile
- Online reputation management (integrations)
- Smart Forms on patient's own device
- Patient communication flexibility rated below average by some users

### Reporting & Analytics
- Curve Business Intelligence (BI): myPractice dashboards
- **6 core KPIs tracked daily:** A/R, Collections, Production, New Patients, Recare, Treatment Plan Value
- Customizable reports
- Multi-location roll-up reporting for DSOs
- Central analytics across all practice sites
- **Limitation:** Getting specific non-standard reports sometimes requires contacting tech support

### AI Features (Curve FLO AI, 2025)
- **Ask CurveAI:** In-platform AI assistant for software questions and training (24/7 chatbot)
- **AI Call Summaries:** Mango Voice integration auto-summarizes patient phone calls
- **Voice-to-Note:** Bola AI dictation for SOAP notes
- **AI X-Ray Diagnostics:** Pearl AI radiograph flagging
- **Eligibility+:** AI insurance verification (95% reduction in verification time; 150% increase in same-day approvals)

### Multi-Location / DSO
- Centralized patient records across all locations
- Centralized billing and eligibility
- Cross-location scheduling
- Multi-site reporting and analytics
- Implementation 360 onboarding for group/DSO rollouts

## User Reviews

### Praise
- Easiest setup and migration — Curve's team handles data conversion
- True cloud = no IT overhead, no servers, no manual updates
- Intuitive scheduling interface; easy to train new staff
- 24/7-adjacent customer support with generally fast response
- All-in-one reduces vendor sprawl
- Constant product updates pushed automatically
- Multi-location management significantly easier than server-based
- AI additions (FLO AI, Eligibility+) well received

### Complaints
- **Imaging is weak** — multiple users compared it unfavorably to Dexis even for basic bitewings
- **Charting is basic** — cannot distinguish restoration materials; no visual differentiation
- **Perio charting described as "messy, confusing, and often inaccurate"** — especially from periodontists
- Cannot see all treatment-planned items on the chart at once; must tab through sections
- **Billing/ledger interface confusing** — especially for inheriting messy billing histories
- **Financial reporting inflexible** — cannot view monthly production by year at a glance; must run each month separately
- Missing features: no time clock, no e-signature on chart, no family credit transfer, no annual insurance max tracker
- **Data migration from Dentrix is problematic** — claims may not convert correctly; some data requires manual re-entry
- Post-sale account rep access declines after onboarding
- **Pricing opacity** — must go through sales for a quote
- Specialty practices find the software insufficient
- Occasional slow performance during peak usage
- No published contract terms

## Integration Ecosystem

Curve operates on a **curated partner model** (fewer, vetted integrations vs. Open Dental's open ecosystem):

| Category | Partners |
|----------|---------|
| Imaging AI | Pearl AI |
| Voice/Clinical AI | Bola AI (Voice-to-Note) |
| Phone/VoIP | Mango Voice (call summaries, two-way texting) |
| Insurance | DentalXChange, Eligibility+ (proprietary) |
| Patient Membership | DentalHQ |
| Analytics/Scheduling | Dental Intelligence |
| Payments | Curve Pay (proprietary); Affirm (BNPL) |

- **API:** Less open than Open Dental; integration partners are selected by Curve rather than open to any developer
- **Trade-off:** Simplicity and curated quality vs. flexibility

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Imaging is the biggest functional gap** — multiple users describe it as inadequate vs. standalone platforms
2. **Charting depth insufficient for specialists** — oral surgery, complex perio, implant workflows not well-supported
3. **Perio charting unreliable** — inaccurate and confusing by periodontist accounts
4. **Billing and ledger UX is confusing** — especially for experienced billing staff
5. **Financial reporting inflexible** — cannot see production across months in one view
6. **Missing basic features** — no time clock, no e-signature on chart, no insurance max tracker
7. **Pricing opacity** — no published pricing; sales call required
8. **Dentrix data migration is notoriously painful** — claims and clinical data often require manual re-entry
9. **Post-sale support declines** — strong pre-sale, weaker ongoing
10. **Internet dependency** — no offline mode; complete outage if internet goes down
11. **Not built for specialties** — general dentistry focus
