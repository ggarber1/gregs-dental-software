# Denticon (Planet DDS)

## Overview

**Company:** Planet DDS, Inc.
**Market position:** ~10–14% cloud market share; 13,000+ practices, 118,000+ users; nearly half of the top 25 DSOs use Planet DDS solutions
**Product:** Denticon (cloud PMS) + Apteryx XVWeb (cloud imaging) + Cloud 9 (orthodontics)
**Capterra rating:** 4.6/5 (110 reviews)
**Architecture:** 100% cloud-based since 2003 — the longest-running cloud dental PMS in the market
**Revenue:** Surpassed $100M ARR in 2024 (40%+ YoY growth); Deloitte Fast 500 #352

---

## Pricing

Planet DDS does **not publish standard pricing**. All quotes via sales reps.

### Published Starting Points
- Estimated starting range: **$280–$795/month** depending on practice size and source
- **Pricing model: per-location** (not per-provider) — a practice pays the same regardless of how many dentists work at one site; advantageous for multi-provider practices

### Launchpad Program (2025) — for emerging groups with 2–9 locations, $2M–$12M revenue
- Three curated technology bundle tiers
- Up to **80% discount** on practice management and imaging in year one
- Includes: Denticon PMS + Apteryx Cloud Imaging + AI

### Add-ons (separate pricing)
- Apteryx XVWeb cloud imaging
- AI Assist (requires XVWeb + Pearl AI)
- AI Voice Perio (launched Feb 2026)
- DentalOS AI Agents (Confirmation Agent, Scheduling Agent — Feb 2026)
- Patient communication modules (SmartAssist, QuickFill)
- Analytics/reporting packages
- RCM/revenue cycle management features

### Implementation/Training
- On-site training: billed at daily rate + travel (airfare, hotel, meals)
- Online/virtual training: billed per hour
- Implementation timeline: ~60 days for solo; ~8 weeks per location for DSOs

### Price Escalation (User-Reported)
Multiple verified reviews document price increases from **$199 → $300 → $400 → $600/month** over time, with no corresponding quality improvements. This is a leading reason users switch away.

## Features

### Scheduling
- Multi-location, multi-provider scheduling
- Family scheduling
- Customizable schedule views
- Online scheduling (patient-facing)
- **QuickFill:** Auto-contacts waitlist patients for last-minute slots
- **SmartAssist:** Tracks unconfirmed appointments and unfilled forms
- **DentalOS Confirmation Agent (Feb 2026):** AI autonomous agent for outbound confirmations — claims 20–50% higher confirmation rates, 15–30% fewer no-shows
- **DentalOS Scheduling Agent (Feb 2026):** Contacts recall/waitlist patients and books directly into PMS

### Clinical
- Intelligent perio charting
- **AI Voice Perio (Feb 2026):** Hands-free voice-powered periodontal charting
- **AI Assist:** Reviews AI imaging opportunities for scheduled patients
- Multi-specialty charting: orthodontic, periodontal, oral surgery, pediatric
- Integration with Apteryx XVWeb for cloud imaging
- AI imaging: **first PMS to integrate all three major AI players** — Overjet, Pearl, and VideaHealth
- In-treatment user flows, referral management

### Billing / RCM
- eClaims submission
- 835 auto-posting
- Central billing office support (manage claims across all locations)
- Insurance eligibility verification via DentalXChange XConnect API
- Automated balance notifications
- Financial campaign automation
- Task manager for billing workflows
- Electronic claims attachments

### Patient Engagement
- Patient segmentation and targeted messaging
- Automated appointment reminders
- Balance reminders / payment nudges
- Two-way text messaging (via Twilio — has experienced reliability outages)
- Document uploads and digital forms

### Reporting / Analytics
- **100+ built-in reports**
- Multi-location dashboards
- Filter by organization, region, or individual office
- Scheduled automated report delivery
- Staff utilization reports
- Financial forecasting
- G2 analytics score: **7.8/10** (notably lower than CareStack's 9.0)

### Imaging
- Apteryx XVWeb: cloud-based, sensor-agnostic, chairside overlay, 3D imaging, accessible from any office
- AI-powered image analysis via Pearl, Overjet, or VideaHealth
- Hybrid cloud/local storage
