# Eaglesoft (Patterson Companies)

## Overview

**Company:** Patterson Companies, Inc.
**Market position:** #2 US market; ~14% global share; ~30,000+ practices
**Product:** Eaglesoft (server-based only; Patterson Fuse is separate cloud product)
**Capterra rating:** ~4.1/5 (152+ reviews)
**G2 note:** 12% more expensive than the average dental PMS product

---

## Pricing

Eaglesoft does **not publish pricing publicly**. All quotes via Patterson Dental reps.

### Historical Model (now transitioning)
- Traditional perpetual license (large upfront fee) + annual Service Club for support
- **Transitioning to subscription-only pricing beginning 2026** — perpetual licenses phased out

### Estimated Monthly Costs (third-party estimates)
| Users | Est. Monthly |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 user | ~$200/month |
| 10 users | ~$1,500/month |
| 100 users | ~$10,000/month |
| 1,000 users | ~$80,000/month |

### Implementation Costs
- Small practices: **$3,000–$10,000**
- Larger practices: **$20,000–$50,000**
- Customization: starting at **~$5,000**

### Hardware Costs (server required)
- Server hardware (small practice): **$3,000–$8,000** + workstations
- Windows Server 2016/2019/2022/2025 required
- Each workstation: Windows 10/11 with .NET Framework
- IT setup: additional cost (varies)

### Eaglesoft Service Club (Annual Support Plan)
- "One low monthly fee" — exact amount not publicly disclosed
- **Price increase confirmed October 2025**
- Includes: unlimited US-based phone/chat/email support (200+ specialists), free software upgrades, Eaglesoft Mobile access, monthly tips, wellness checks, waived Patterson CarePay+ subscription (first year, with 12-month commitment)

## Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop appointment scheduling
- Color-coded appointment types and provider views
- QuickFill: fill open slots from waitlist
- Custom time slots and recurring appointments
- Automated recalls via Communication Manager (text/email)
- Block scheduling for providers and operatories

### Clinical
- Clinical charting with perio templates
- Periodontal charting
- Treatment planning with financial estimates for patients
- Patient consent forms integrated with treatment plans
- Insurance coverage estimation at treatment planning
- CAESY patient education integration

### Imaging
- Eaglesoft Advanced Imaging (built-in module)
- Compatible with 50+ imaging devices/technologies: DEXIS, VixWin, Cliniview, MiPACS, Apteryx, Schick, and many more
- **Pearl Second Opinion AI integration** (July 2024): real-time AI radiologic detections rotate with images in Advanced Imaging
- IOSS integration (replaces CDR DICOM in v24.20)
- **Known limitation:** Imaging resolution stuck in older standards; cannot save HD images or use HD cameras in older configurations

### Billing & Financial
- Line-item accounting
- MoneyFinder: identify missed revenue opportunities
- Electronic claims via Vyne Trellis and DentalXChange
- eClaims status tracking; can move eClaims to "open" status (v24.20+)
- ADA 2024 dental claim forms (v24.20+)
- Automated payment posting
- Patient payment processing via Patterson CarePay+
- Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) suite

### Patient Engagement
- Communication Manager: automated texts/emails for recalls, post-op instructions, account balances, appointment reminders
- YAPI integration: paperless forms, intra-office messenger, automated confirmations
- Demandforce integration: patient communication automation
- Legwork PRM: patient reviews and web presence
- ReachAll Dental Recall: automated recall and marketing

### Reporting
- Standard production, collections, and A/R reports
- MoneyFinder report for revenue opportunity analysis
- **Customization described as "limited"** by multiple users
- Appointment display options (employer, insurance, patient info in popup)

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Private general dentistry practices, solo through multi-provider group
- **~30,000 practices** use Eaglesoft
- **Sweet spot:** Mid-sized practices (2–10 operatories) already in the Patterson supply chain ecosystem
- **Hard limitation:** Does NOT support multiple offices on a single shared database — single-location per installation
- Patterson Fuse (separate cloud product) is the answer for multi-location needs
- Primarily general dentistry; limited specialty-specific depth

## User Reviews

### Praise
- Familiar and functional for long-term users; intuitive layout for front desk staff
- Strong imaging device compatibility (50+ technologies)
- Good scheduling flexibility and drag-and-drop interface
- QuickFill valued for filling gaps
- Tight Patterson ecosystem integration (one vendor for hardware, supplies, software)

### Complaints
- **"Outdated before you even use it"** — UI/UX described as 10–15 years behind
- **Not cloud-based** — requires physical server; high IT burden
- **"Development reportedly stalled — changes today are mainly cosmetic"**
- **Imaging resolution problems** — "stuck in the 80s; can't save HD images or use HD cameras"
- **No multi-location support** — hard blocker for growing practices
- **Customer support severely lacking** — bugs deferred to next update rather than fixed
- Limited reporting customization
- Slow and crash-prone in some environments
- Steep learning curve due to non-intuitive interface
- Glitches discovered by users before Patterson proactively communicates them

---

## Customer Support

- **Channels:** Phone, live chat, email; 200+ US-based specialists (Service Club)
- **Hours:** Business hours only; limited after-hours support
- **Reality:** Polarized reviews — some "beyond amazing"; many others describe as "severely lacking"
- AI phone system is a frustration barrier to reaching technicians
- Bug resolution is slow — often told to "wait for the next release"
- No proactive communication about known glitches
- Third-party support options exist (Dillon Dental Networks, IAG-USA) for responsive help

## Contract Terms

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Not cloud-based** — #1 complaint; fundamental competitive disadvantage
2. **Single-location only** — no shared database across offices; hard ceiling for growth
3. **Technology stagnation** — core architecture and UI are 10–15 years behind market
4. **Imaging HD support broken** — cannot use HD cameras/images
5. **Poor support responsiveness** — bugs deferred; no proactive communication
6. **Inflexible reporting** — limited customization
7. **Opaque total cost** — no public pricing; sales rep required
8. **Patterson supply chain lock-in** — software and supply pricing entangled
9. **Transitioning business model** — uncertainty for perpetual license holders
10. **No teledentistry** — missing an increasingly reimbursed care modality
