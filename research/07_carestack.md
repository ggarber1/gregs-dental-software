## Pricing

CareStack does **not publish standard pricing**. Aggregator estimates: ~$650–$698/month starting.

### Pricing Model
- **Per-provider** (or per-location, or per-user — flexible). A 4-provider practice pays roughly 2x a 2-provider practice at the same location. This distinguishes CareStack from Denticon's per-location model.
- **3 months free** for new clients (promotional, as of 2025)

### Implementation Fee
- Charged in some cases depending on data migration complexity
- Industry benchmark range: $5,000–$25,000 for complex setups
- "No other additional charges" beyond implementation fees per FAQ

### Onboarding
- Structured 4–8 week onboarding program
- Weekly 60-minute sessions with operations staff
- CareStack University (CSU): 24/7 on-demand training video library
- Go-live: ~60 days for existing practices; a few weeks for de novo

### Add-on Products
| Product | Details |
|---------|---------|
| CS Conversations | AI-powered VoIP phone system (built on VoiceStack) |
| CareRevenue | Outsourced RCM managed services (separate product line) |
| CS Membership | $99/month up to 60 members; then $2/member/month — native in-house membership plan management |
| VoiceStack | AI phone system with conversation AI, call analytics, automated follow-up |

## Features

### Scheduling
- Daily Practice Snapshot: production, collection, and schedule utilization per location
- Month-at-a-glance view with empty chair visibility
- Multi-provider scheduling with availability settings per provider
- Multi-location scheduling with interactive map for patient location selection
- Online scheduling (patient-facing)
- Schedule gap optimization tools
- Automated appointment reminders

### Clinical
- HIPAA-compliant charting
- **AI-assisted clinical notes** (voice-to-text, 2025)
- Referral management: tracks inbound/outbound referrals; automated updates to referring GPs
- Teledentistry support
- Electronic prescriptions
- Specialty-specific workflows: ortho, perio, oral surgery, endo, pediatric
- AI imaging integrations: **Overjet, Pearl AI, Denti.ai, Aeka** (four AI partners — more than any competitor)
- Integrated with Apteryx, DEXIS, SOTA Cloud Imaging

### Billing / RCM
- Electronic claims with **>90% first-pass acceptance rate**
- Auto-posting
- Credit card processing (funds post directly to ledger)
- Central Billing Office (CBO) support
- A/R management: claimed **40% reduction in days-in-A/R**
- Insurance eligibility verification
- Electronic claims attachments
- CareRevenue: fully outsourced RCM managed service

### Patient Engagement
- Automated payment reminders
- Two-way texting
- Patient portal (some reported access code friction)
- CS Conversations: AI phone with call tracking, transcription, automated follow-up on missed calls
- Patient financing via Sunbit integration
- Reputation management via BirdEye integration

### Reporting / Analytics
- Real-time dashboards and scorecards
- Multi-location, multi-provider tracking
- Financial, production, and appointment-based reports
- **Customizable enterprise dashboards** (key reason Espire Dental, 25+ locations, switched to CareStack)
- G2 analytics score: **9.0/10** (significantly higher than Denticon's 7.8)

### Imaging
- Sensor-agnostic: integrates with Apteryx, DEXIS, SOTA Cloud, and others
- AI imaging: Overjet (Smart Dental Platform), Pearl AI, Denti.ai, Aeka
- **Notable gap: CBCT imaging capture not supported**

### Native Products (built-in, not add-ons)
- **CS Membership:** In-house membership plan builder — a genuine differentiator
- **VoiceStack:** AI phone system with real-time transcription and analytics
- **CareRevenue:** Fully outsourced RCM managed service

## User Reviews

### Praise
- Responsive customer support — chat support resolves issues within minutes
- **Constantly soliciting and acting on user feedback** — features shipped ~3 months after request
- All-in-one platform reduces vendor sprawl
- Excellent onboarding and training support
- Strong analytics and enterprise dashboards
- **Native membership plan management** is a differentiator
- Users feel heard by the product team

### Complaints (in order of frequency)
1. **Too much information on a single page** — interface is overwhelming; not intuitive
2. **Too many steps for simple tasks** — must right-click → "view dental chart" instead of double-clicking
3. **Steep learning curve** — significant training required before staff are productive
4. **Front-office-centric design** — providers/clinical staff find it creates friction in their workflows
5. **Specialty workflows are workarounds**, not native — built for general dentistry
6. **CBCT imaging capture not supported**
7. **Patient billing statements are confusing** — hard for patients to read
8. **Patient addresses don't carry over** with records — manual re-entry required
9. **No unlimited outbound SMS/email** — larger offices hit caps
10. **WiFi-dependency leaves practices stranded** during internet outages
11. Some backend settings not user-configurable — must go through CareStack support

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Overcrowded UI** — tries to do too much on one page; high training requirement
2. **Clinical workflow friction** — designed for front office, not providers
3. **Specialty practice limitations** — oral surgery, endo, ortho require workarounds (DSN Software actively markets against this)
4. **No CBCT capture** — gap for implant-heavy and multi-specialty practices
5. **WiFi-only** — complete loss of functionality during internet outages
6. **Patient billing clarity** — statements hard for patients to read
7. **Per-provider pricing** — expensive at scale for high-provider-count locations
8. **Backend settings locked** — some configuration requires CareStack support intervention
9. **SMS/email caps** — no unlimited messaging option
10. **Data migration quality** — patient addresses and some records require manual cleanup post-migration
11. **No native mobile app** — only browser-based access
