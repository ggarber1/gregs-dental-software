# Practice-Web (Practice-Web, Inc.)

## Overview

**Company:** Practice-Web, Inc. (El Dorado Hills, CA; founded 1988 — one of the oldest dental software companies in the US)
**Market position:** Key market player (Grand View Research); 1,700+ active practices; popular with solo practitioners and startup practices
**Architecture:** Primarily server-based; cloud option available but problematic
**G2 rating:** 4.9/5
**Capterra:** Highly rated (user satisfaction reported 96–98%)
**TechRadar assessment:** "Decent, middle of the road option — fair value"

---

## Pricing

Practice-Web is one of the more transparent vendors, though add-on costs can obscure the true total.

### Base Pricing
| Plan | Monthly |
|------|---------|
| Base/Core | **$149/month** |

**What's included at base:** Clinical charting, scheduling, billing, treatment plans, insurance, reporting, paperless office tools.

### Add-On Pricing (confirmed)
| Add-On | Monthly |
|--------|---------|
| pwConnect (Two-Way Texting, up to 2,000 outbound/month) | $79/month |
| pwReviews (Online Reviews via Text + Email) | $49/month |
| eRx (Electronic Prescribing) | Additional (price not publicly disclosed) |
| CallerID Smart package | Additional |
| Patient newsletters / email marketing | Additional |
| Online scheduling | Additional |
| Teledentistry | Additional |
| iPad Patient Forms | Part of Plus bundle |

### Bundle
"Plus" package bundles pwConnect + pwReviews + iPad Patient Forms at a discount (exact price not publicly disclosed).

### Implementation Costs
- Small practices: starting from ~$1,000
- Larger enterprises: up to $10,000+

### True Cost Reality
Practice-Web explicitly markets itself as "a fraction of the price of Dentrix/Eaglesoft." The $149 base is genuinely low, but a fully functional practice needs $128–$250+ in add-ons, bringing realistic total to **$277–$400/month**.

## Features

### Scheduling
- Fully electronic scheduling
- Find openings quickly at a glance
- Operatory-level scheduling views
- Appointment confirmation via text and email (pwConnect)
- Automated reminders
- Automated schedule updates when confirmations received
- No-show reduction tools via integrated texting

### Clinical
- **Innovative 3D tooth charting** (visual treatment planning from 3D model — a standout differentiator)
- Restorative charting
- Periodontal charting with **voice perio** (hands-free solo charting — praised by hygienists)
- Graphical perio chart for monitoring patient progress over time
- Treatment planning with prioritization, fee display, and insurance coverage breakdown
- Electronic patient signature on treatment plans
- Digital forms/intake
- Prescription management (eRx as add-on)
- Customizable clinical templates
- Electronic Health Records (EHR)

### Billing & Collections
- Patient billing and insurance billing
- Insurance claims management
- Electronic claims via DentalXChange
- Real-time eligibility checks (DentalXChange)
- Electronic attachments
- Collections tools via TSI integration
- Payment processing via Global Payments — pay-by-text included
- Patient statements (print and electronic)

### Patient Engagement
- Two-way texting (pwConnect — add-on)
- Online review solicitation (pwReviews — add-on)
- Mass email/newsletter marketing (add-on)
- Online appointment scheduling (add-on)
- Patient portal
- Teledentistry (add-on)
- Automated appointment reminders (text + email)

### Reporting & Analytics
- **60+ built-in reports**
- Production and income analysis by date range, provider, or clinic
- Multi-location reporting support
- Dental Intelligence integration for advanced analytics (third-party)

### Practice Management / Operations
- Interoffice messaging
- Operatory alerts
- Task assignment and tracking with completion tracking
- Inventory management
- Referral tracking
- Document management
- User access controls (role-based)
- Multi-location support

### Imaging
- Integration with third-party imaging software (no proprietary hardware)
- Compatible partners: Sopro, Yapi, Apteryx XVWeb
- No proprietary sensor line (unlike DentiMax)

## Target Market
- **Primary:** General dentistry solo and small-to-mid group practices
- **Positioning:** Value-oriented practices wanting full features at lower cost than Dentrix/Eaglesoft
- **Multi-location:** Supported; marketed to group practices
- **DSO:** Not a primary play; limited scalability for enterprise
- **Specialties:** General dentistry focused; no specialty-specific modules
- **Geographic:** US-only (California-headquartered)
- **Historical:** Practices migrating from legacy DOS-era or expensive server-based systems

## User Reviews

### Praise
- **Extremely responsive customer support** — phones answered; rare hold times
- Easy to learn; intuitive after initial setup
- **Perio charting with voice feature praised by hygienists**
- **3D tooth charting is a standout visual differentiator**
- Affordable entry price vs. Dentrix/Eaglesoft
- Treatment planning interface praised
- Long-term customer loyalty (users staying 5–10+ years)
- Add-ons enhance workflow (texting, reviews)
- Partner integrations work smoothly (Dental Intelligence, Weave)
- Weekend support availability confirmed by multiple users

### Complaints
- **No mobile app (iOS or Android)** — most cited weakness; complete gap in 2024/2025
- **Dated graphical interface** — needs modernization
- **Cloud version is slow/laggy** — up to 5-minute lag; defeats purpose
- Texting has word count limitations
- Texts appear from 1-800 number, not a local number (reduces patient response rates)
- **Recall list reporting described as inaccurate**
- Statement printing process is cumbersome
- Everything is a separate charge — "nickel-and-dime" pricing
- HMO insurance handling issues reported
- Initial training described as inadequate
- Occasional slowness (may require software/hardware updates)

## Customer Support — A Genuine Competitive Advantage

This is Practice-Web's most consistently praised attribute:

- **Channels:** Phone (primary), email
- **Self-reported satisfaction:** 98–100% post-support survey for several consecutive years
- **User-confirmed:** "Unlike other software support, they actually pick up calls" (December 2024 verified review)
- **Callbacks:** Quick when not immediately available
- **Weekend support:** Multiple users confirm weekend availability
- **Assessment:** Practice-Web's support contrasts sharply with DentiMax's phone queue and Eaglesoft's bug deferral culture

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **No mobile app (iOS or Android)** — the single most cited weakness; complete gap in 2024/2025
2. **Dated interface** — UI/UX has not kept pace with modern design standards
3. **Cloud version is unreliable** — 5-minute lag; defeats purpose of cloud
4. **Texting limitations** — word count caps; 1-800 sender number reduces response rates
5. **Recall list inaccuracies** — affects core hygiene reappointment workflow
6. **Nickel-and-dime pricing** — base is stripped; every add-on costs extra
7. **eRx is a paid add-on** — considered standard by many competitors
8. **HMO billing issues** — complex managed care plans cause problems
9. **Initial training insufficient** — learning curve higher than claimed
10. **No free trial** — hard to evaluate before buying
11. **Limited DSO scalability** — not built for enterprise/multi-location at scale
12. **No dedicated specialty modules**
13. **No meaningful AI features or roadmap visible**
