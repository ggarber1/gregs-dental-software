# Dentrix (Henry Schein One)

## Overview

**Company:** Henry Schein One (subsidiary of Henry Schein, Inc.)
**Market position:** #1 by installed base in the US — ~48,000+ practices; ~18% global market share
**Products:** Dentrix G7 (server-based, legacy) + Dentrix Ascend (cloud-based)
**Capterra rating:** ~4.0/5 (hundreds of reviews)

---

## Pricing

Dentrix does **not publish pricing publicly**. All quotes are via Henry Schein sales reps.

### Dentrix G7 (Server-Based)
- Typical monthly cost (license + support): **$500–$700+/month** for a solo practice
- Per-incident support (no plan): **$179.95/incident**
- Server hardware + IT setup: **$2,000–$10,000** for solo; up to **$20,000+** for larger offices
- Windows Server 2016/2019/2022 required (8GB+ RAM, 80GB+ disk, RAID storage)

### Dentrix Ascend (Cloud)
- One-time enrollment fee: **$995**
- Database migration fee: **$750 per database**
- Monthly subscription (estimates): **$399–$500/month** (~10 concurrent users); **$1,200–$1,600/month** for larger practices
- Additional 5-user blocks: **~$250/month**
- Built-in imaging add-on: **$200/month** (used by ~90% of customers)
- Patient engagement / Demandforce: additional monthly fee
- eServices (eClaims, eligibility, ePrescribe): bundled or à la carte
- Implementation: **$2,000–$5,000** (small practices); **$10,000–$20,000** (100+ users)

**Three Ascend tiers:** Starter, Essentials, Complete — exact tier pricing not published.

**True cost reality:** Add-ons required for a fully functional system make total cost 1.5–2.5x the base subscription price.

## Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop appointment scheduling
- Smart scheduling automation with AI-assisted booking
- Automated appointment reminders (text/email)
- Online scheduling (patient self-booking)
- Real-time insurance eligibility verification at booking
- Color-coded appointment types and operatory views
- Route slips (Ascend limitation: cannot print more than 3 days in advance)

### Clinical
- Digital charting (Dentrix Chart)
- Quick Exam for streamlined documentation
- Voice Notes: hands-free dictation for charting
- Image Verify: AI-assisted X-ray quality checking at point of capture
- Dentrix Detect AI: AI-powered caries/pathology detection (via Videa Health)
- Treatment planning with case presentation tools
- Electronic prescribing (ePrescribe)
- Perio charting and periodontal templates
- CDT code management (2025 codes included)

### Imaging
- Dentrix Smart Image: integrated imaging, 150+ compatible devices
- One-click image capture, auto-coding, cloud storage (Ascend)
- DICOM-compatible
- AI diagnostics via Detect AI / Videa Health AIDE
- Direct integration with iTero intraoral scanners (2024)

### Billing & Insurance
- Electronic claims (eClaims) via DentalXChange / Vyne
- Electronic remittance advice (eEOB) and automated payment posting
- Insurance eligibility verification (Eligibility Pro — 191M checks in 2025)
- Billing statements and collections tools
- Line-item accounting
- Patient payment processing

### Patient Engagement
- Automated reminders and confirmations (text/email)
- Digital intake forms — AI-assisted (22M forms completed in 2025)
- Patient portal
- Two-way texting (via Demandforce add-on)
- Email marketing and review solicitation (add-on)
- Recall management

### Reporting & Analytics
- Power Reporting module (widely criticized as non-intuitive)
- Production, collection, and A/R reports
- Morning Huddle dashboard
- Multi-location consolidated reporting (Ascend)
- Dental Intelligence integration (analytics add-on)

### AI Features (2025–2026)
- Detect AI / Videa Health AIDE: radiograph pathology detection
- Image Verify: X-ray quality check at capture
- Eligibility Pro: automated insurance verification at scale
- Digital Forms: AI-powered license/insurance card scanning
- Claire Support Agent: AI-powered 24/7 support chatbot
- Agentic platform architecture (March 2026 announcement for DSOs)

## User Reviews

### Praise
- Comprehensive, mature feature set
- Most dental staff already know Dentrix — minimal retraining
- Strong treatment planning and charting
- Insurance eligibility automation saves significant staff time
- Ascend praised for remote access capability

### Complaints
- **UI described as "feels like software from 10+ years ago"**
- Steep learning curve for new users
- **Power Reporting is universally criticized** as confusing and non-actionable
- Dentrix Ascend: treatment plans calculate incorrectly; many insurance plans not supported for eClaims/attachments; cannot send preauth to secondary insurer
- Billing ledger gets messy from auto insurance adjustments
- **High total cost of ownership** — every feature costs extra
- Long support wait times
- Complex navigation between patient sections

## Contract Terms

- **Ascend:** Monthly subscription; cancel anytime; no refund for pre-paid periods
- **G7:** Traditional perpetual license + annual support renewal
- **No published minimum term** — but contracts negotiated through reps may include 1-year minimums
- **Data portability concern:** Exporting data requires Dentrix-specific tools; migration is costly

- **Platform:** Henry Schein One API Exchange

- **Volume:** 22+ certified partners on Partnerbase
- **Key integrations:** Demandforce, Birdeye, Dental Intelligence, Weave, Legwork, Align/iTero, DentalXChange, Vyne, Patient News

## Key Weaknesses Summary

1. **Reporting (Power Reporting)** — universally criticized
2. **Ascend still feature-incomplete** vs. G7 — treatment plan bugs, no secondary preauth
3. **High total cost** — add-ons for everything
4. **Vendor lock-in** — expensive to migrate away
5. **UI modernization slow** — dated vs. Curve Dental, CareStack
6. **No native iOS/Android app** for Ascend
7. **Support cost model** — pay per incident without a plan
